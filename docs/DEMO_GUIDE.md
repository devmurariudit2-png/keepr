## 🎬 The 4-Step Demo Flow (10 Minutes Total)

### Step 1: The "Drowning in Leads" Problem (2 Mins)
* **Action**: Show the main Dashboard. Point to the stats (`Today's Leads`, `Qualified`, `Pending`).
* **Script**: 
  > *"As a broker, you probably pay Zillow, Facebook, or local ads for leads. But 90% of them are cold, unqualified, or looking for a rental. If your team is manually calling all of them, they are wasting hours dialing wrong numbers. Keepr acts as your digital ISA (Inside Sales Agent). It instantly texts every lead, qualifies them, and schedules them into your calendar, so you only talk to serious buyers."*

### Step 2: The Qualification Chat (3 Mins)
* **Action**: In the dashboard table, click on **Chloe Green** (Status: `qualifying`).
* **Script**:
  > *"When Chloe came in from Facebook, Keepr instantly texted her. Look at this chat history. The AI naturally asked about her budget, location, and timeline. The moment she replied with $450k and a 2-month moving window, the AI updated her lead details on the right: Budget, Area, Timeline, and Financing."*
* **Action**: Point to the **Lead Score ring** (45/100).
  > *"Keepr calculates a deterministic score from 0 to 100 based on their budget, timeline, and financing status. No guesswork. Your team can sort the dashboard by score and target the highest-value buyers first."*

### Step 3: Interactive Live Test (3 Mins)
* **Action**: Open a new tab to the homepage **[https://keepr-delta.vercel.app](https://keepr-delta.vercel.app)**. Scroll to the **Self-Demo Widget** and share your screen.
* **Script**:
  > *"Let's test it live. Give me a name and what you're looking for, and we can watch the AI qualify you in real-time."*
* **Action**: Fill out the widget with their inputs. Have a quick 2-3 turn conversation with the AI chat bubble (e.g., *"Looking for a 3-bedroom place in Marina Heights"*). 
* **Action**: Switch back to the Dashboard tab, refresh, and show them how the lead card is updated live with the score.

### Step 4: The Hook - One-Click Booking (2 Mins)
* **Action**: Open **Isabella Lewis** (Status: `qualified`). Click **Book Appointment**.
* **Script**:
  > *"Once a buyer is fully qualified, the AI sends them your calendar link. Here is the integrated scheduler (Cal.com iframe). The lead picks a slot, confirms the meeting, and Keepr automatically updates their status to 'Appointment Booked'. The broker doesn't have to dial a single number."*

## 🎯 The Close (Ask this verbatim)
> *"If this worked exactly like this on your real leads starting next week, would you be open to trialing it with a list of 20 of your past leads?"*

* **Rule**: If they say "yes, but...", write down the exact feature they say is missing. Do not build it until 5+ independent brokers ask for the same thing!
